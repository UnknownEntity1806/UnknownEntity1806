About BamBam

Bambam came into my life when he was already 11 years old (in 2021). His former owner had passed away. The family could not take care of him, as Bambam had been mistreated and did not fully trust other humans nor dogs.

I was already looking for a senior dog, because they often have the smallest chance of finding a new home. And I really wanted to provide a senior with a loving,kind and quiet life.

I am happy that BamBam was able to trust me and became a very loving dog towards me. He lived his last years in peace and comfort. And he provided me with a lot of emotional support and joy when I reached my lowest points in life.I am very grateful for the time we had together and I will always cherish the memories we made.

Rest in Peace
2010 - 2025
