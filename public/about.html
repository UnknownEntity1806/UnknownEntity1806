Why this website?

Who is the webmaster?

Hello! I'm Malou, the creative mind behind this website. I'm a digital artist from the Netherlands, diagnosed with autism in 2024. I have a passion for designing stationery and creating digital art, which I love to share with others through my website.
I created this site as a personal project to showcase my work and connect with others who share similar interests. I believe that everyone deserves a space to express themselves creatively, and I hope that my website can be a source of inspiration and joy for those who visit it.

You may contact me through email.

What is it with Unknown Entity 1806?

Unknown Entity 1806 (it) was created as I searched for a new online identity in 2024.
It is not just an original character, but a persona I created to represent myself online.
I used to be known online as Studio Louma. However I struggled with my creativity, as well as just life in general at the time. I felt restricted in what I could create with my online presence. So I wanted a new one. Coincidentally I received my autism diagnosis around the same time. This inspired the creation of my new persona as a weird little alien/robot that did not understand themselves, nor was understood by others; who felt like they were just a number in the world.
It has antennas to signify its sensitivity to the world around.
