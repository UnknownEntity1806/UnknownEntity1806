About @its Cloudy Day

@its Cloudy Day is a character created by Unknown Entity 1806. Her actual name is Chloe Dae , which sounds similar to Cloudy Day, which is her social media handle.

Chloe is a recent high school graduate who is set to become an influencer. She is a cheerful and optimistic person who enjoys making others smile. Through her social media presence, she hopes to spread positivity and brighten people's days.

    She loves:

  • the colour baby blue
  • looking at the clouds
  • reading historical novels
  • baking sweets

I created this OC because I wanted to create a character in a more real-world setting. She allows me to practice drawing more realistic outfits, etc.
